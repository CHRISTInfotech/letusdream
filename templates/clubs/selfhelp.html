Self-Help Club
About
Focusing on health and happiness, this club targets personal development and self-discovery. To achieve this goal, participants explore inspirational ideas from multiple and diverse sources. The focal point is on considering and celebrating the beauty in caring and being mindful of living happier and healthier lives.
Objectives
● Prioritize discussions and activities that contribute to physical and mental well-being, fostering a holistic approach to health and happiness.
● Discuss and encourage the adoption of positive habits that contribute to overall well-being, both physically and emotionally.
● Create awareness around mental health and provide a safe space for open discussions on mental well-being.
