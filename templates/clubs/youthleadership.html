Welcome to the Global Youth Leadership Network!

The Global Youth Leadership Network (GYLN) is an innovative platform designed to empower emerging youth leaders from various DREAMS Chapters across the globe. Founded in November 2020, GYLN serves as a nurturing space for personal growth, leadership development, and meaningful networking among aspiring individuals.
