Our Purpose

In a world increasingly shaped by AI and automation, we risk losing touch with the very essence of our Humanness - empathy, responsibility, connection and creativity.

HUMAN stands for:

Harmony, Unity, Mindfulness, Action for Nurturing

This Youth-led leadership workshop nurtures the development of value-based leadership among youth through the 17 United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). This one-day transformative leadership workshop incorporates music, dance, theatre and storytelling to promote the essence of Humanness.

Developed under the Let Us Dream, in collaboration with CHRIST (Deemed to be University), Pune Lavasa Campus, this initiative aims to engage potential student leaders from over 100 academic institutions across the Globe. Through curated workshops and performances, participants will be mentored and empowered to become SDG Youth Ambassadors and peer-change agents who will lead The HUMAN Movement.
