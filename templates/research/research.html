Research Areas

Explore our comprehensive research domains that drive innovation and positive change

DREAMS Intervention

Understanding how our program transforms students' self-perception, social skills, and leadership abilities. We analyze the impact on school participants, college peer mentors, and senior leadership team members.

  • Personal development assessment
  • Leadership skill evaluation
  • Community impact analysis

Community Conference

Evaluating the effect of conferences on local communities, understanding cultural differences, and analyzing diverse collaborative models from a global perspective.

  • Cultural diversity studies
  • Social entrepreneurship skills
  • Community initiative growth

Professional Club

Analyzing the impact of our one-year mutual mentoring club meetings on members' personal, social, and leadership skills across different countries and populations.

  • Holistic development assessment
  • Work-life balance analysis
  • Cross-cultural comparison

Music Band

Investigating how music band intervention transforms students' self-perception, social and leadership skills, and analyzing the transformation among the audience.

  • Creative expression impact
  • Leadership through arts
  • Audience transformation

Global Youth Leadership Training

Examining how the Young Community Leadership Program (YCLP) impacts youth leaders' effectiveness in creating authentic and united communities through international sessions.

  • Youth leadership effectiveness
  • Community unity building
  • Inclusive mindset development

Current Research Projects

Peer Mentor Experiences Study

Analyzing the experiences of peer mentors in the DREAMS Intervention Program and their journey in mentoring others.

Leadership Skill Development Analysis

Investigating growth and leadership skill experiences of peer mentors participating in the DREAMS Intervention Program.

Comparative Efficacy Study (PhD Research)

A comprehensive comparative study on the efficacy of DREAMS Educational Intervention Program versus PERMA PRP Educational Intervention Program.
