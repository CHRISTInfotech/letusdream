Sustainability Goals | Let Us Dream
We envision a global family collaborating to create a sustainable world. Our holistic approach covers various programs aligned with the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs). Let Us Dream stands committed to fostering positive change.

DREAMS Intervention Program

SDG 4 - Quality Education
Differentiating ourselves with an impactful 1:2 mentor-mentee ratio ensures a deep understanding of concepts. Engaging activities bridge formal education and real-world experiences, empowering students to become lifelong learners and critical thinkers. Self-initiation is encouraged through activities like theatre skits.
SDG 5 - Gender Equality
Let Us Dream summer camps create a safe, inclusive environment where mentees learn through diverse activities, fostering unity, fellowship, and valuable life skills, promoting participant respect and collaboration.
SDG 11 - Sustainable Cities and Communities
As advocates for community empowerment, Let Us Dream focuses on sustainable living and urban development. Our programs use sustainable resources, teaching communities to care for the world. We aim to build an extensive network for education and best practices in sustainable living.
SDG 17 - Partnership for the Goals
Our DREAMS Intervention Program has expanded globally through collaborations with like-minded communities, establishing a network working towards the SDGs.

Let Us Dream Clubs

SDG 10 - Reduced Inequality
Let Us Dream Clubs provide a safe space, fostering inclusivity and understanding among individuals from diverse backgrounds.
SDG 11 - Sustainable Cities and Communities
Monthly gatherings of Let Us Dream Clubs contribute to community engagement, aligning to create sustainable and resilient communities.

Let Us Dream Fellowship

SDG 3 - Good Health and Well-Being
Addressing psychosocial needs, the fellowship contributes to mental well-being and community health, aligning with SDG 3.
SDG 4 - Quality Education
The Let Us Dream Fellowship empowers young leaders through interdisciplinary training, aligning to provide quality education beyond traditional settings.
SDG 16 - Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions
By developing fellows for a transformational impact, Let Us Dream aligns to promote peace, justice, and strong institutions.
