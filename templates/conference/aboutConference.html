Let Us Dream Community Conferences
The Let Us Dream (LUD) Conferences are a powerful initiative dedicated to fostering community empowerment through compassion, collaboration, and service. What began as a local gathering in Monroe, Louisiana, has grown into a global movement, comprising both annual community conferences and triennial international conferences.
Annual Conferences
Make a difference together! Attend our annual conferences held around the world.
Connect with volunteering opportunities. ⦁ Build your social and professional network. ⦁ Create a space to share ideas and skills. ⦁ Learn from leading, unsung heroes tackling problems, big and small.
Triennial Conferences
Connect globally, act locally! Our international conference happens once every three years. It brings together service initiatives from around the world.
Collaborate and network with like-minded people. ⦁ Learn how to make a positive impact in your own community. ⦁ Celebrate unsung heroes.
History of LUD Conferences
The first LET US DREAM conference was launched in 2017 to inspire community service. From then on, all conferences organized have provided a platform to learn, share, and celebrate acts of volunteerism. The first Triennial International Conference was conducted virtually in 2020 during the pandemic. It highlighted resilience and strength, showcasing community initiatives from around the world. The conference targets both younger and older generations, introducing them to various community service opportunities and models. Through discussions and presentations, attendees gain insights and recommendations for positive community development. Currently, conferences are organized around the world. This includes multiple locations in India and USA, and countries like Germany, Nepal, South Africa, and more.

To provide an opportunity to share, learn and support more community intervention research, theories and models.
To provide a forum for sharing experiences including the successes and challenges in community organisations and efforts.
