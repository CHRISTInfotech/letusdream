HISTORY

  • The first LET US DREAM conference was launched in 2017 to inspire community service.
  • From then on, all conferences organized have provided a platform to learn, share, and celebrate acts of volunteerism.
  • The first Triennial International Conference was conducted virtually in 2020 during the pandemic. It highlighted resilience and strength, showcasing community initiatives from around the world.
  • The conference targets both younger and older generations, introducing them to various community service opportunities and models.
  • Through discussions and presentations, attendees gain insights and recommendations for positive community development.
  • Currently, conferences are organized around the world. This includes multiple locations in India and U.S.A., and countries like Germany, Nepal, South Africa, and more.

VISION

Empowering local communities for sustainable global impact and improvement.

MISSION

To provide an opportunity to share, learn and support more community intervention research, theories and models.

To provide a forum for sharing experiences including the successes and challenges in community organisations and efforts.

To invite individuals with bigger hearts and deeper thoughts for their society to be a support system for the community empowerment initiatives.

OBJECTIVES

To offer the participants an opportunity to bridge the knowledge gap through the interaction of researchers and practitioners worldwide.

To make recommendations towards achieving greater global peace and harmony worldwide.

To provide an opportunity to share, learn, and support more community interventions research, theories, and models.

To provide a forum for sharing experiences including the successes and challenges in community organizations and efforts.

To invite individuals with bigger hearts and deeper thoughts for their society to be a support system for the community empowerment initiatives.

IMPLEMENTATION

The LET US DREAM conferences work in the following ways:

  • Connect with universities, communities, companies, NGOs, and other institutions to bring you impactful conferences.
  • Choose from annual community conferences or international triennial conferences held around the world.
  • Attend panels, network with fellow professionals, volunteer your time and skill set and acknowledge unsung heroes serving the community.

TARGET AUDIENCE

Be the change you wish to see! The LET US DREAM conferences welcome:

  • Adult and young-adult volunteers.
  • Business owners with community development projects.
  • Non-profit organizations, NGOs & philanthropists.
  • Board members, social workers, and educators interested in collaboration.
  • College students and professors.
  • Anyone passionate about community initiatives and leadership.
